I am a graduate student at the University of Waterloo (Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering). My current research is on microseismic monitoring in hydraulic fracturing applications. There is limited knowledge of the mechanisms behind microseismic event generation. I'm currently using the eXtended Finite Element Method (XFEM) to model a shear dilation crack and the resulting wave propagation. I will then use this computational model to analyse the microseismic response due to shear dilation.
